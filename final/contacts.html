FoA is a non-profit, internationals organization Animal advocacy organization incorporated in the state of New York since 1957.
FoA's goal is to free animals from cruelty and institutionalized exploitation around the world.

The organization has grown from its beginnings as the most comprehensive low-cost spay neuter program in the country, facilitating more than 2.8 million spay/neuter procedures to date.
